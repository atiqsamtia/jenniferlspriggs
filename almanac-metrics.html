Almanac Metrics

Optimizing the urban farming experience.

Almanac Metrics

Deliverables: Logo

Timeline: 3 Weeks

Tools: Illustrator

Almanac Metrics is a personalized platform built for urban farmers that gives them a bird's eye view of their harvest and materials. For this project the client wanted something that felt modern and wasn't too overtly rustic. We also wanted to explore adding in visual themes that played up the metrics side of this business, playing with subtle nods to mathematical motifs.

First Concepts

Final Touches & Adding Color

The client was a huge baseball fan and loved the feel of the retro hand lettering. We decided to go in that direction, and incorporate a smaller icon into the lockup as well. With the logo having two parts to it's whole, the icon could sit by itself on marketing collateral and still feel holistic to the Almanac brand.

The color palette was inspired by old imagery of the Farmer's Almanac. I wanted to stand for a dusty red feeling overall, punctuated with the pop color of the soft yellow.
