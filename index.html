About

I am a product designer and illustrator living in Seattle Washington.

Trained deep in the exotic Ohio Valley at the University of Cincinnati’s DAAP Design program with a focus in fashion, I discovered my passion for graphic and surface design. A healthy interest in interactive and web design, drawing from my dark past as a webmaster for a teen boyband fansite, has contributed to my ability to take on a variety of projects and challenges.
