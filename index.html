    About me.

    I'm Jenn! I'm a Visual UX Designer and Illustrator living in Seattle, Washington.


    Trained deep in the exotic Ohio Valley at the University of Cincinnati’s DAAP Design program with a focus in fashion, I discovered my passion for graphic and surface design. A healthy interest in interactive and web design, drawing from my dark past as a webmaster for a teen boyband fansite, has contributed to my ability to take on a variety of projects and challenges.


    I've worked for big companies like Procter & Gamble, LPK, American Eagle and Abercrombie and Fitch. I've spent time consulting with small companies and startups like Lulabop and Dokonoko trying on all of their hats. If you have a good hat to wear, I'd like to try it on! I'm open for new projects and experiences so if you think you have something cool, you can contact me here.
