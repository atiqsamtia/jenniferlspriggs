Go Go Travel

Agent Portal Redesign

Role: Junior Designer

Lead: Edwin Rogers

Tools: Omnigraffle, Photoshop, Illustrator

Project Completed: July 2015

Summary & Concept

GOGO Worldwide Vacations is the largest privately owned leisure travel chain in the United States that serves over one million customers annually. They currently have over 170 stores in 14 states.

GOGO Travel's agents operate through a booking engine to quickly secure their clients their dream vacation. Working with Softvoyage, it was our job to re-vamp the entire engine to be clean, responsive, and intuitive.

My Role

As the junior on this project it was my responsibility to wireframe pages and deploy the style guide across all screen mockups for the desktop design.

Visual Design


I love spending my time making vector illustrations in Adobe and that was the strength I brought to my team for this project. We were unsure about how to approach the plane chart challenge brought to us by our clients, but I jumped at the chance to push some pixels.

It was entirely unexpected that we would have time to touch that part of the project and it was a huge client delight. They loved the color-coordination and visual key.
